Bronco Burger



Description

The manly man burger (not for wimps), great for NFL tailgating.


Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb bacon, chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced jalapeños
  • 1/4 cup diced onions
  • 1/4 cup BBQ sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and pickles for toppings

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground beef, chopped bacon, cheddar cheese, jalapeños, onions, BBQ sauce, salt, and pepper.
  3. Form the mixture into patties.
  4. Grill the patties for about 5-7 minutes on each side or until cooked to your liking.
  5. Toast the burger buns on the grill for a minute.
  6. Assemble the burgers with lettuce, tomato, pickles, and any additional toppings you like.
