Mexican Pizza



Description

Mexican Pizza is a delicious fusion of Mexican and Italian flavors, featuring a crispy tortilla crust topped with seasoned ground beef, beans, cheese, and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 2 large flour tortillas
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 can refried beans
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/2 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 tsp taco seasoning
  • Olive oil for brushing
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add taco seasoning, salt, and pepper to the beef. Stir in refried beans until well combined.
  4. Brush one side of each tortilla with olive oil and place them on a baking sheet, oiled side down.
  5. Spread the beef and bean mixture evenly over one tortilla.
  6. Top with salsa, cheddar cheese, mozzarella cheese, diced tomatoes, black olives, and green onions.
  7. Place the second tortilla on top and brush the top with olive oil.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the tortillas are golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  9. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing into wedges.
