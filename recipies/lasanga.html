Lasanga



Description

Lasanga is a traditional Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Marinara sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fresh basil
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and garlic, and sauté until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in marinara sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, parsley, and salt.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  8. Add a layer of noodles, followed by a layer of ricotta mixture, then mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with meat sauce and mozzarella on top.
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  11. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil.
